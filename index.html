MedME
is a prescription management app intended to remind users when it is time to take their medication. This collaborative application was developed by Matt McCarthy, Dirk Wiggins, Matthew Wright and Joe McCreesh.

To develop MedME the team utilized HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, JavaScript, JQuery, Firebase, Twilio, and various API's such as Google Maps, National Institue for Health and the FDA.

Friend finder
is a social app aimed at locating friends with common interests. The user answers a few questions and is matched with another user with common interests. This was an independant project.

To develop Friend Finder a full stack site, I utilized HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, JavaScript, JQuery, Node.js, Express-Node, Body-Parser-Node, Path-node, GitHub and Heroku.
